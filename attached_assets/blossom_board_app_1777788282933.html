✦ Everything you need

Features that spark joy ✨

Everything you'd expect from a pro whiteboard, wrapped in the cutest pink UI you've ever seen.

✏️
Freehand Drawing
Smooth bezier-curved strokes with variable width, colour picker, and pressure simulation. Like drawing on real paper.
📌
Cute Sticky Notes
Pop in pastel notes with emoji, auto-resize text, spring animations, and a folded-corner style. 6 adorable colours.
🔷
Shapes & Connectors
Rectangle, ellipse, line, and arrow tools. Shift-lock to perfect squares and circles. Layer and group elements freely.
🔍
Zoom & Pan
Mouse wheel zoom from 10% to 400%. Space-drag to pan. Fit-to-screen in one click. Canvas is infinite.
👥
Collaborate with Friends
Mock live cursors, participant avatars, typing indicators, and a real-time activity feed — all beautifully simulated.
📅
Notion-style Task Board
Kanban, Calendar, and List views. Drag-and-drop cards. Priority badges, due dates, assignees, and filters.
↩️
Undo / Redo
Full 50-step history stack. Ctrl+Z your mistakes away. Every action is tracked with a friendly toast notification.
🖼️
Export Anywhere
Download your board as PNG. Works on the full canvas or just your current selection. Pure client-side, no upload needed.
💾
Auto-Save
Your board saves to localStorage every 10 seconds. Come back tomorrow — your work will still be there, waiting.
